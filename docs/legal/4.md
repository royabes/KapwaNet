# 1️⃣ Paid Professional Directory Policy

*(Community Directory – Not a Marketplace)*

## Purpose

The Professional Directory exists to:

* Help community members **find local professionals**
* Allow professionals to **support the sustainability of the sharing platform**
* Maintain a **clear separation** from volunteer bayanihan help

The directory is **informational only**.

---

## What the Directory Is

✔ A listing of professionals and service providers
✔ A visibility and discovery tool
✔ A way for professionals to support the platform through listing fees

---

## What the Directory Is NOT

❌ A booking system
❌ A hiring or contracting platform
❌ A service marketplace
❌ An endorsement or certification program

The platform does **not**:

* Arrange services
* Set prices
* Guarantee work
* Verify credentials unless explicitly stated

---

## Eligibility for Directory Listings

Professionals may list if they:

* Are legally permitted to offer their services in Alberta
* Accurately represent their qualifications
* Agree to comply with all applicable laws
* Understand that listing does not imply endorsement

---

## Listing Information (Allowed)

Directory profiles may include:

* Name or business name
* Service category
* Description of services
* Credentials (self-declared)
* License number (optional, self-entered)
* Service area
* Website or contact information
* Community support statement (optional)

---

## Prohibited Content

Listings must not include:

* False or misleading claims
* Guarantees of outcomes
* Claims of platform endorsement
* Illegal or unlicensed services
* Pricing presented as platform-approved

---

## Platform Disclaimer

> Professional listings are provided for informational purposes only.
> The platform does not verify credentials, endorse providers, or facilitate services.
> Any engagement occurs independently between users and providers.

---

## Enforcement

The platform reserves the right to:

* Remove listings that violate this policy
* Suspend or terminate accounts
* Update policies as required by law

# 2️⃣ Subscription & Listing Terms

*(Minimal Fees for Platform Sustainability)*

## Purpose of Fees

Fees paid by professionals are **not service fees**.

They are:

* Directory listing fees
* Visibility or placement fees
* Contributions toward platform maintenance, moderation, and hosting

---

## What Fees Cover

Depending on tier, fees may include:

* Directory listing visibility
* Featured placement
* Profile customization
* Analytics (views, clicks)
* Community supporter badge

---

## What Fees Do NOT Cover

Fees do **not**:

* Guarantee leads or work
* Influence ratings or reviews
* Represent commissions or revenue sharing
* Create any employment or agency relationship

---

## Sample Subscription Structure (Illustrative)

| Tier                  | Fee             | Purpose                 |
| --------------------- | --------------- | ----------------------- |
| Community Supporter   | Low monthly fee | Basic directory listing |
| Featured Professional | Moderate fee    | Highlighted visibility  |
| Community Sponsor     | Higher fee      | Advertising + listing   |

(Exact pricing to be defined by the platform.)

---

## Advertising Disclosure

All paid listings will be clearly labeled as:

* “Paid Listing”
* “Sponsored”
* “Community Supporter”

> Paid listings are advertisements.
> The platform does not endorse or guarantee advertised services.

---

## Payments & Taxes

* Fees are billed on a recurring or fixed-term basis
* Receipts will be provided
* Applicable taxes will be charged as required by law
* No refunds for unused listing time unless required by law

---

## No Employment or Agency Relationship

Payment of fees does **not** create:

* Employment
* Partnership
* Agency
* Contractor relationship with the platform

---

## Governing Law

These terms are governed by the laws of **Alberta, Canada**.
